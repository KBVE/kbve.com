AutoZone, Inc. (NYSE: AZO) is the leading retailer and distributor of automotive replacement parts and accessories in the Americas, serving both do-it-yourself (DIY) customers and professional service providers. Founded in 1979 and headquartered in Memphis, Tennessee, AutoZone has built the largest automotive aftermarket retail chain in the United States, with operations extending across the Americas through multiple retail formats and distribution channels.

With over 7,000 stores across the United States, Mexico, and Brazil, AutoZone serves millions of customers annually through its extensive retail network, commercial delivery programs, and online platforms.
