ADP - Human Capital Management Technology Leader

Section titled “ADP - Human Capital Management Technology Leader”

Automatic Data Processing, Inc. (NASDAQ: ADP) is a leading global provider of cloud-based human capital management (HCM) solutions, serving over 1.1 million clients worldwide with comprehensive payroll, human resources, benefits administration, and workforce management technology. Founded in 1949 and headquartered in Roseland, New Jersey, ADP has evolved from a payroll processing company into a comprehensive human capital management technology platform serving businesses of all sizes.

With a market capitalization exceeding $124 billion and approximately 64,000 employees, ADP processes payroll for one in six workers in the United States and serves clients across more than 140 countries. The company’s mission is to power human achievement by delivering comprehensive HR and payroll solutions that enable organizations to unlock their full potential through better workforce management.

Employer Services

Cloud-based platforms and HR outsourcing solutions for businesses of all sizes:

  • ADP Workforce Now: Mid-market HCM solution for businesses with 50-1,000 employees
  • ADP Vantage HCM: Enterprise-grade HCM platform for large organizations (1,000+ employees)
  • Time and Attendance: Workforce management and time tracking solutions
  • Talent Management: Recruiting, onboarding, performance management, and learning solutions
  • Benefits Administration: Employee benefits enrollment and administration platforms
