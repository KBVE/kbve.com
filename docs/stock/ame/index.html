AME - Precision Instruments and Electromechanical Solutions Leader

Section titled “AME - Precision Instruments and Electromechanical Solutions Leader”

AMETEK, Inc. (NYSE: AME) is a leading global provider of electronic instruments and electromechanical devices, serving customers across aerospace, defense, process, medical, and industrial markets with differentiated technology solutions. Founded in 1930 and headquartered in Berwyn, Pennsylvania, AMETEK has built a portfolio of specialized businesses that occupy leading positions in niche markets aligned with secular growth trends.

With a market capitalization exceeding $41 billion and approximately 21,500 employees worldwide, AMETEK operates through two differentiated business segments: Electronic Instruments Group (EIG) and Electromechanical Group (EMG). The company’s success is built on its disciplined AMETEK Growth Model, which integrates operational excellence, new product development, global and market expansion, and strategic acquisitions with a focus on cash flow generation and disciplined capital deployment.

Differentiated Operating Groups

Section titled “Differentiated Operating Groups”

AMETEK operates through two complementary business segments serving specialized markets:

Electronic Instruments Group (EIG)

Section titled “Electronic Instruments Group (EIG)”

Advanced analytical, test, and measurement instrumentation:

  • Process Instrumentation: Analytical instruments for oil and gas, petrochemical, pharmaceutical, and semiconductor industries
  • Aerospace and Defense: Aircraft and engine sensors, power supplies, embedded computing, and data acquisition systems
  • Power and Industrial: Power quality monitoring devices, uninterruptible power supplies, and programmable power equipment
  • Laboratory Equipment: Precision instruments for ultra-precision manufacturing, medical, and test and measurement markets
  • Materials Analysis: X-ray diffraction, thermal analysis, and materials testing equipment
  • Environmental Monitoring: Air quality monitoring, emissions measurement, and environmental compliance instruments

Electromechanical Group (EMG)

Section titled “Electromechanical Group (EMG)”

Precision motion control, specialty materials, and thermal management:

  • Precision Motion Control: High-precision servo motors, drives, and motion control systems for automation and robotics
  • Specialty Metals: High-purity powdered metals, specialty alloys, and metal matrix composites
  • Medical Components: Single-use surgical instruments, implantable components, and drug delivery systems
  • Aerospace Components: Motor-blower systems, heat exchangers, and thermal management solutions
  • Electrical Interconnects: Engineered electrical connectors and electronics packaging solutions
  • Technical Motors: Specialized motors for commercial appliances, industrial equipment, and aerospace applications
