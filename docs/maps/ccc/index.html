Capital City of Clouds

CCC

The Capital City of Clouds, or CCC, is a historical city characterized by its towering, green-clad skyscrapers and advanced technology, serving as a foundational pillar within our gamesâ€™ lore. Often called Cloud City Capital, its blend of cyberpunk aesthetics and solarpunk sustainability shapes the narrative and environment throughout the game.
